A few tips for getting the most out of the map.

## Clusters

Points are grouped into numbered **clusters** so the map stays fast even with
hundreds of thousands of points. Click a cluster to zoom in; keep clicking until
you reach individual points.

## Named vs. show all

By default the map shows only **named** places — the ones with a real name in
OpenStreetMap. Use the toggle in the top-right corner to **show all**, including
unnamed entries. Your choice is remembered the next time you visit.

## The detail panel

Click any point to open the detail panel. It pulls a description from
**Wikipedia**, a photo from **Wikimedia Commons** (or **Mapillary** as a
fallback), the place's OpenStreetMap details, and — where available — its
**Google rating**. A reviews section is coming soon.
