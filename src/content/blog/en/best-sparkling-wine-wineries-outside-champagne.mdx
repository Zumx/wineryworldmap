Champagne sells more bottles than any other sparkling appellation,
but it is far from alone. Spain, Italy, England, the US, Australia,
South Africa, and a handful of others all produce serious
traditional-method (or *charmat* alternative) sparkling. These are
the best estates outside Champagne.

## Italy: Franciacorta

Italy's leading traditional-method sparkling region, in Lombardy.
Ca' del Bosco, Bellavista, Berlucchi, Ferghettina, and Cavalleri are
reference Franciacorta producers; the *Satèn* style (Chardonnay
only, lower pressure) is the regional specialty.

## Italy: Trento DOC

Trentino's traditional-method DOC. Ferrari (the dominant brand), Cesarini
Sforza, and Letrari are reference producers; cooler than Franciacorta,
slightly more austere wines.

## Spain: Cava

Penedès-centred traditional-method sparkling. Recaredo, Gramona, and
Llopart in the new Corpinnat group (which broke away from Cava DO);
Raventós i Blanc (now Conca del Riu Anoia DO). Cava's recent Reserva
and Gran Reserva tiers signal serious quality.

## England: Sussex and Hampshire

The fastest-growing sparkling region in the world. Nyetimber, Gusbourne,
Hambledon, Hattingley Valley, Wiston, and Ridgeview produce
traditional-method sparkling from Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, and Pinot
Meunier that has consistently beaten Champagne in blind tastings.

## US: California

Schramsberg (Calistoga), Iron Horse (Sonoma), Roederer Estate
(Anderson Valley, sister to Louis Roederer), Domaine Carneros (sister
to Taittinger), and Mumm Napa are reference US sparkling producers.

## Tasmania, Australia

Tasmania's cool climate gives Australia's best traditional-method
sparkling. Jansz, Arras, Stefano Lubiana, and Pirie are reference
producers; many bottle vintage cuvées that age beautifully.

## South Africa: Méthode Cap Classique

The traditional-method sparkling category. Graham Beck, Pongrácz,
Le Lude, and Silverthorn are reference producers; Krone (Twee
Jonge Gezellen) is the historic name.

## Loire (Crémant de Loire)

A serious alternative — Bouvet-Ladubay, Langlois-Château, and
Domaine de Saint-Just produce Crémant of high quality at a fraction
of Champagne prices.

## Burgundy (Crémant de Bourgogne)

The other serious alternative — Bailly-Lapierre, Veuve Ambal, and
Louis Bouillot are reference Crémant de Bourgogne producers.
