Most Champagne is made by *négociants-manipulants* — houses buying
fruit from many growers. The *récoltant-manipulant* (RM) makes
Champagne from their own grapes, often from a single village or
vineyard. The RM movement has reshaped the perception of Champagne
since the 1990s, with growers now sitting at the top of every
serious sommelier's list.

## Jacques Selosse, Avize (Côte des Blancs)

The most influential grower of the modern era. Anselme Selosse's
oxidative, *vins de lieu* approach to Grand Cru Côte des Blancs sites
changed what was possible in Champagne. Effectively impossible to
visit.

## Egly-Ouriet, Ambonnay (Montagne de Reims)

A Pinot Noir-led RM with Grand Cru holdings in Ambonnay and Bouzy;
long lees ageing and an unusually deep, vinous style.

## Pierre Péters, Le Mesnil-sur-Oger (Côte des Blancs)

A reference Blanc de Blancs grower with the Cuvée Spéciale Les
Chétillons single-vineyard wine; the standard against which all Le
Mesnil RMs are measured.

## Agrapart & Fils, Avize (Côte des Blancs)

A leading Avize biodynamic grower; Avizoise, Mineral, and the
Vénus single-vineyard are reference Côte des Blancs RMs.

## Larmandier-Bernier, Vertus (Côte des Blancs)

A biodynamic Côte des Blancs grower with single-vineyard
Vieille Vigne du Levant and Les Chemins d'Avize bottlings.

## Vouette et Sorbée, Buxières-sur-Arce (Aube)

A leading southern grower; Bertrand Gautherot's biodynamic Aube
Pinot Noir RMs (Fidèle, Blanc d'Argile) helped legitimise the region.

## Cédric Bouchard / Roses de Jeanne, Aube

The cult Aube grower. Single-vineyard, single-vintage, single-
village wines that broke every Champagne marketing rule.

## Bérêche et Fils, Ludes (Montagne de Reims)

A modern Montagne de Reims grower making distinctive single-village
and single-parcel cuvées; among the most internationally
celebrated of the current generation.

## Chartogne-Taillet, Merfy (north of Reims)

A small RM that revived an obscure village; old-vine Pinot Meunier
and Chardonnay bottlings that have become reference points.

## Tarlant, Oeuilly (Vallée de la Marne)

A long-established RM in the Marne Valley known for un-dosed
Champagnes and single-parcel cuvées (La Vigne d'Or, Cuvée Louis).
